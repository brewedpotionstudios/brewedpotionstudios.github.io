Who We Are

Brewed Potion Esports Solutions Inc.

Brewed Potion is the premiere esports technology startup in the Bicol region. We are incubated by Project TALUBO and BiCoRSE at Bicol University. We build the digital infrastructure that powers high-stakes live events.

Originally founded in 2023 to revitalize in-person esports competitions, we have evolved into a software-first logistics provider. We translate the high-octane demands of professional esports into enterprise-grade stability for corporate and government events.

Vision

To set the national standard for live event technology. We aim to make professional-grade esports infrastructure accessible to every Filipino community, establishing the region as a global contender in event tech.

Mission

To replace operational chaos with digital precision. We empower organizers with tools that secure data, slash administrative costs, and automate workflows—building a sustainable, professional foundation for the Philippine esports ecosystem.

Grassroots First

Community is our Core. We build technology designed to professionalize the grassroots scene, turning casual local gatherings into structured, competitive ecosystems for players of all levels.
